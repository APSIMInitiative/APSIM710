Core Training Materials

If you are new to Apsim, start with these documents. They will teach you the basic skills required to construct a simulation and how to work with the results, including graphing. As they build upon the content in previous documents, it is recommended that they be completed in order.

1 - Introduction to Apsim UI
2 - Surface Organic Matter
3 - The Nitrogen Cycle
4 - Sowing A Crop
5 - Long Term Simulations
